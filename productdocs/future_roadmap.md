# Future Roadmap

This document captures features and requirements that are necessary for the long-term vision but are currently out of scope for the MVP / active sprints.

## Logistics & Delivery
- [ ] **Complex Multi-Leg Routing**: Support for routes with multiple stops that may involve different legs or complex optimization (e.g., TSP solvers). Currently, we assume "1 Delivery Route = 1 Truck Run".
- [ ] **Dynamic Re-routing**: Ability to change route order mid-flight based on traffic or cancellations.
- [ ] **Vehicle Capacity Planning**: Hard constraints on weight/volume per vehicle to prevent overloading.
