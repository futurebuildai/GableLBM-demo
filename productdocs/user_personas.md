# User Personas & Workflows

## 1. "Counter Carl" (Inside Sales / Counter Rep)
**Role:** Manages walk-in traffic and phone orders for contractors.
**Goals:** Speed, Accuracy, Inventory visibility.
**Pain Points:** 
- System too slow to look up prices while customer waits.
- Can't tell if items are *actually* in the yard or just "in the computer".
**UX Needs:**
- Keyboard-driven interface (mouse is too slow).
- "Quick Add" command palette.
- Instant visibility of "Available to Promise" inventory.

## 2. "Yardmaster Yvonne" (Yard Foreman/Picker)
**Role:** Manages the physical inventory, loading trucks, and receiving.
**Goals:** Accuracy of picks, safety, yard organization.
**Pain Points:**
- Paper pick tickets get wet/lost/illegible.
- Doesn't know a truck is coming until it arrives.
**UX Needs:**
- **Mobile First:** High-contrast tablet interface (outdoor use).
- **Big Buttons:** Usable with gloves on.
- Map view of the yard for item location.

## 3. "Dispatch Barnaby" (Logistics Manager)
**Role:** Scheules trucks, assigns drivers, plans routes.
**Goals:** Maximize truck utilization, On-time delivery.
**Pain Points:**
- Manual whiteboard scheduling is chaotic.
- Last-minute "hot shot" delivery requests.
**UX Needs:**
- Drag-and-drop Gantt chart for truck scheduling.
- Map-based route planner.

## 4. "Controller Connie" (CFO/Office Manager)
**Role:** Manages AR/AP, General Ledger, Payroll, Credit.
**Goals:** Cash flow management, Risk mitigation (Liens), Accurate reporting.
**Pain Points:**
- Month-end close takes huge amount of meaningful time.
- Chasing contractors for payment.
**UX Needs:**
- Dashboard view of "Cash Position" and "Aging AR".
- Automated alert workflows for credit hold.

## 5. "Contractor Ken" (The Customer)
**Role:** Builds houses, orders materials.
**Goals:** Getting materials on time, correct pricing, easy billing.
**Pain Points:**
- Waiting on hold to place an order.
- Invoices getting lost in the mail.
**UX Needs:**
- **Customer Portal:** Self-service ordering, invoice history, payment portal.
