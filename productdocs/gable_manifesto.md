# The GableLBM Manifesto: Why We Build

To the Dealers, the Yardmasters, and the Visionary Builders:

For too long, the LBM industry has been held captive by "Legacy Moats." Software vendors have prioritized their own fees over your operational freedom. They have built silos where you needed bridges. They have given you tools that require a PhD to operate, while your real-world labor is revolving faster than ever.

**GableLBM exists to end the era of the "Feature Trap."**

We believe:
1.  **Ownership is Non-Negotiable:** Your core operational software should be yours to own, audit, and extend. Open Source is the only way to ensure 50-year business continuity.
2.  **Complexity is a Failure of Design:** If an 18-year-old on their first day can't pick a ticket or sell a stud within 15 minutes, the software has failed.
3.  **Real-Time is the Only Time:** In a volatile market, a "Nightly Sync" is a death sentence. Information must flow like the materials themselves—continuously and visibly.
4.  **AI Must Be Alpha, Not Fluff:** We don't use AI for "summarizing emails." We use it to count windows on blueprints, to find rusty flashing in the shed, and to load trucks in the perfect LIFO sequence.
5.  **Integration is a Right, Not a Privilege:** Connecting your ERP to your website, your truck trackers, or your power tools should be free and easy. No more "Integration Taxes."

GableLBM is not just another ERP. It is an **Open Operating System** for the future of the construction supply chain. 

**Let’s build. Better. Faster. Open.**
