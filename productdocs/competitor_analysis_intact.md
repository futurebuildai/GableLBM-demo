# Competitor Analysis: Intact iQ (GenetIQ)

## Overview
Intact iQ (marketed as GenetIQ in the US) is a modern, modular ERP focused on flexibility and "fit." Their primary differentiator is the "GenetiQ Nexus" workflow engine.

## Key Feature Sets

### 1. The GenetiQ Nexus (Workflow)
- **"Your rules, your way"**: Highly customizable workflow engine.
- **Manage by Exception**: Automation of routine tasks with alerts for deviations.
- **Extensiblity**: Easy creation of custom fields and rules without core code changes.

### 2. Industry Specifics (LBM/Merchant)
- **Lumber Pack Tracking**: Detailed tracking of tagged lumber packs.
- **Trade Counter POS**: Optimized for high-speed counter transactions.
- **Units of Measure**: Flexible handling of linear, square, and cubic units.

### 3. Mobile & Accessibility
- **Native Apps**: Dedicated iOS and Android apps for sales and warehouse.
- **Cloud-Native Browser UI**: No legacy desktop client required.

### 4. BI & Reporting
- **GenetiQ Analyst**: Inbuilt pivot-table style data analysis.
- **Power BI Integration**: Direct pipes for advanced visualization.

## Strategic Observations
- **Marketing Highlight**: "Future-Proof." They sell the idea that their software evolves with the dealer.
- **Alignment with Gable**: Their focus on "Merchant Sovereignty" (though not using that word) through customization is direct competition for our "Sovereignty" mission.
- **Gable Differentiator**: Gable is Open Source; Intact is a proprietary "Black Box" that you customize via their proprietary tools.
