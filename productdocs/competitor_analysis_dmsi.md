# Competitor Analysis: DMSi Agility

## Overview
DMSi Agility is a legacy powerhouse in the LBM (Lumber & Building Materials) industry. It is positioned as a comprehensive, end-to-end ERP that "covers every inch of the business."

## Key Feature Sets

### 1. Inventory & Warehouse Management
- **Dimensional Support**: Tracks dimension records (Species, Grade, Treatment) under single item codes.
- **Multi-UOM**: Support for PC, UNIT, BF, MBF, LF, and M3.
- **Real-time Sync**: Tracks inventory across multiple branches live.
- **Mobile Yard/Warehouse**: Handheld solutions for picking and receiving.

### 2. Sales & Commerce
- **Digital OrderPad**: Mobile-first counter sales tool.
- **Escalator Pricing**: Automated scheduled price increases on quotes (critical for long-term construction).
- **Millwork Configurator**: Advanced logic for non-stock assembled items.
- **eCommerce**: Integrated B2B/B2C portals for customer self-service.

### 3. Logistics & POD
- **Truck Optimization**: Load planning and dispatch tools.
- **Proof of Delivery (POD)**: Captures GPS, signatures, and photos at the jobsite.

## Strategic Observations
- **Marketing Highlight**: "Tailor-made" for LBM. They focus on the specific complexities of lumber.
- **The "Tax" Model**: High cost of entry and significant fees for integrations/APIs.
- **Messaging Weakness**: Legacy tech stack feel. Framing it as "The system you have to live with" rather than "The system you want to build with."
