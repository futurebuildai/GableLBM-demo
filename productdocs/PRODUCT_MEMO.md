# GableLBM: Product Memo & Strategic Vision

**Date:** February 2026
**Status:** Living Document
**Audience:** Marketing, Sales, Product, & Stakeholders

---

## 1. Executive Summary

**GableLBM** is the first **Open Operating System** designed specifically for the Lumber & Building Materials (LBM) industry. We are not building just another ERP; we are building a platform for **operational sovereignty**. 

In an industry held captive by "legacy moats"—software vendors that prioritize their own fees over dealer innovation—GableLBM offers a radical alternative: **high-performance, AI-native, open-source software that dealers can own, not just rent.**

Our mission is to end the era of the "Feature Trap" by providing tools that are intuitive enough for a new hire to master in minutes, yet powerful enough to run a $100M+ multi-site operation in real-time.

---

## 2. The Problem: The Legacy Moat & The Feature Trap

The LBM software landscape is dominated by legacy players (BisTrack, Spruce, Agility) that suffer from three critical failures:

1.  **The Usability Crisis**: Systems are "feature-rich but utility-poor." They have added buttons for 30 years without removing any, leading to software that feels like a kaleidoscope of grey menus. This creates high cognitive load, effectively gatekeeping the industry from the next generation of talent.
2.  **Data Silos**: Information is locked away in obscure database schemas. Connecting your ERP to your website, your truck trackers, or your power tools requires expensive "integration taxes" or is simply impossible.
3.  **Vendor Lock-in**: Dealers are "renters" of their core operational data, dependent on slow-moving vendor roadmaps for even the smallest changes.

---

## 3. The Solution: GableLBM

We believe in a future where:
*   **Real-Time is the Only Time:** Inventory, sales, and logistics data must flow instantly.
*   **Ownership is Non-Negotiable:** Dealers should have the right to own, audit, and extend their code.
*   **Complexity is a Failure of Design:** If an 18-year-old can't pick a ticket or sell a stud within 15 minutes, the software has failed.

### The "Open Operating System"
GableLBM is built as an open platform. The core is open-source (MIT/Apache style), allowing dealers to:
-   **Own the Code:** Eliminate the risk of vendor sunsetting or acquisition.
-   **Innovate Faster:** Build custom "Apps" on top of the core without waiting for us.
-   **Share Solutions:** A dealer who builds a custom DeWalt battery tracker can share it with the community.

---

## 4. Key Differentiators & Innovation Vectors

### A. AI-Native Commerce (The "Smart Counter")
Most "AI" in ERPs is just a chatbot wrapper. GableLBM is AI-native:
*   **Visual Recognition:** The system "sees" the items on the counter or in the yard.
*   **Contextual Pricing:** Instead of static lookups, the system suggests the *winning* price based on real-time market conditions and customer history.
*   **Document Parsing:** We don't just store blueprints; we read them. Our Vision Pipeline automatically counts windows, studs, and headers from PDF plans to generate quotes in seconds.

### B. The Unified Yard (Geospatial Ops)
We treat the physical lumber yard as a digital twin.
*   **Real-Time Tracking:** Every forklift and pallet is a node on the network.
*   **Smart Dispatch:** Picking isn't just a status update; it's a live-tracked activity.
*   **Autonomous Logistics:** The system suggests load orders that maximize profit per mile and minimize truck wear.

### C. Consumer-Grade UX ("Industrial Dark")
We reject the notion that B2B software must be ugly. Our "Industrial Dark" aesthetic is designed for high contrast and readability in warehouse environments, with a fluidity that rivals modern consumer apps.

---

## 5. Core Modules

### 1. Sales & Order Entry ("Counter")
*   **Speed:** < 60 seconds from "Hello" to "Ticket".
*   **Omnibar Search:** Cmd+K to find customers, SKUs, or orders instantly.
*   **Pricing Waterfall:** Automatic logic handling Job Overrides > Contracts > Tier Pricing > Retail.

### 2. Inventory Management ("The Pile")
*   **True Lumber Logic:** Native handling of Board Feet (MBF), Linear Feet (LF), and Pieces simultaneously.
*   **Cycle Counting:** Mobile-first flows for daily inventory verification.

### 3. Logistics & Dispatch
*   **Zone Printing:** Auto-routes pick tickets to the correct yard zone.
*   **Proof of Delivery (POD):** Mobile capture of photos, signatures, and GPS timestamps at the job site.

### 4. Financials ("Ledger Lite")
*   **Real-Time Job Costing:** Every transaction is tagged to a specific project.
*   **Instant AR:** "Total Exposure" views for credit managers.

### 5. Tech Admin Panel
*   **Self-Service API:** Instant API key generation for internal dev teams.
*   **Webhooks:** No-code event triggers (e.g., "When Order > $10k, Slack the GM").

---

## 6. Strategic Go-to-Market

Our model disrupts the traditional SaaS "Rent-Seeker" approach:
1.  **Open Core:** The software is free.
2.  **MSP Revenue:** We monetize through "Managed Service Provider" packages (Hosting, Security, Support).
3.  **Co-op Strategy:** We empower Buying Groups to "fork" GableLBM and offer their own branded ERP to members, creating massive distribution leverage.
