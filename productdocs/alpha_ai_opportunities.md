# Alpha AI Opportunities: Beyond the Hype

As product researchers, we must distinguish between "Chatbot Fluff" and "Operational Alpha." These are 10x opportunities that legacy systems simply cannot implement without a total rewrite.

## 1. The "Vision-to-BOM" (Takeoff Automation)
**Status Quo:** A rep looks at a 50-page PDF blueprint and manually types in every window, door, and beam. Error rate is ~10%.
**Alpha Opportunity:** 
- **The Disruptor:** A native Vision model that doesn't just "read" text, but "understands" construction symbols. 
- **Utility:** Drop a PDF -> GableLBM generates a **Draft BOM (Bill of Materials)** with high/low confidence flags. The rep's job shifts from "Typing" to "Verifying."

## 2. Dynamic Lead-Time Forecasting (The Supply Chain Prophet)
**Status Quo:** Lead times are static fields (e.g., "Windows = 4 Weeks").
**Alpha Opportunity:**
- **The Disruptor:** AI that monitors vendor confirmation patterns and actual receipt dates.
- **Utility:** If a specific Andersen facility is trending 2 days late across the industry, GableLBM auto-adjusts customer promises *proactively*. This builds massive trust with contractors.

## 3. "Customer Health" Predictive Score
**Status Quo:** Credit holds are binary (Over limit = Stop).
**Alpha Opportunity:**
- **The Disruptor:** Sentiment analysis on communication + payment trend jitter.
- **Utility:** "Contractor Mike usually pays in 28 days. This month he's at 35, and his last 3 emails were 'curt.' Suggest a proactive check-in before he hits his limit." This prevents bad debt before it happens.

## 4. Multimodal Yard Search (The "Shed Scout")
**Status Quo:** Searching for an item requires knowing the SKU or exact name.
**Alpha Opportunity:**
- **The Disruptor:** Semantic search on physical attributes + visual similarity.
- **Utility:** A yard guy doesn't know the code for a "rusty-colored flashing cap." He describes it or takes a picture of a scrap piece. GableLBM identifies it instantly via Vector Search and Image Embedding.

## 5. Automated "Load Optimization" (3D Bin Packing)
**Status Quo:** Loaders decide "what goes where" based on intuition.
**Alpha Opportunity:**
- **The Disruptor:** A 3D packing algorithm constrained by LIFO (Last-In, First-Out) for delivery stops.
- **Utility:** The system shows the loader a 3D diagram on their tablet: "Put these 40 studs on the bottom left, the windows in the rack, and the drywall on top (with a tarp)." This reduces damage claims and delivery time at the job site.
