# Feature Gap Analysis & GableLBM Roadmap

Based on research of DMSi Agility and Intact iQ (GenetIQ), the following features are identified as critical gaps for GableLBM to achieve market parity and then leapfrog legacy incumbents.

## 1. High Priority Gaps (Market Parity)

### Escalator Pricing Logic
- **Competitor Benchmark**: DMSi Agility.
- **Requirement**: Ability to schedule price adjustments on quotes for long-term projects.
- **Gable Edge**: Integrate with live market indices (Random Lengths, Fastmarkets) to automate these escalators based on real-time volatility.

### Item Configurator (Millwork)
- **Competitor Benchmark**: DMSi Agility.
- **Requirement**: A rules-based configurator for non-stock items (Doors, Windows, specialized millwork).
- **Gable Edge**: Use AI-vision to verify configurator outputs against blueprint specs.

### B2B/Contractor Portal
- **Competitor Benchmark**: Intact iQ / DMSi.
- **Requirement**: A self-service portal for contractors to download invoices, check statements, and place re-orders.
- **Gable Edge**: **White-labeled Sovereign Portal**. The dealer owns the portal and the data, it's not a platform-locked portal.

## 2. Strategic Leapfrogs (Differentiation)

### The "Governance" Advantage
- **Differentiator**: While Intact iQ has "Nexus" for customization, Gable has **RFC Governance**.
- **Message**: Dealers don't just "request features" from a vendor; they **vote on and fund the roadmap.**

### AI-Native "Smart Counter"
- **Differentiator**: Legacy ERPs focus on "Mobile OrderPads" (just screens on tablets).
- **Message**: Gable uses LLMs and Vision AI to *input* the order from a picture of a material list or a conversation, reducing "Quote-to-Ticket" time by 80%.

### Zero-Tax Integration
- **Differentiator**: Epicor and DMSi charge $5k-$15k for API access.
- **Message**: Gable is Open-by-Default. Connect anything for free.
